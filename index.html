INTRODUCTION
Hey there! Welcome to Hon + Bun! This is the page where we write about the food that we've tried in different places around the world. We also try recipes that we find online, review them, and rate them.

To learn more about who we are, click the "About Us" section. To see where we've eaten and our recipe reviews, click the "Recommendations" button. To check out our social media pages, click the icons on the bottom left.

ABOUT US
She's Hon. He's Bun. Together, we're Hon + Bun!

Hon works in student affairs. Bun is a software engineer. A pair of Yelp Elites, we're always looking for the next best thing that food has to offer. We like all kinds of food: popular, hole-in-the-wall, you name it. We don't discriminate and we're coming to find you and eat you. Delicious food, you can run but you can't hide.

How did we come up with the name Hon + Bun? Well, we love nicknames! When we first started talking, we dabbled in a couple different nicknames but when we heard the words hon and bun, we knew we found the ones. They sound so well together that we thought it would be a good title for our page!

We love food and love to write about it. We hope that you find our recommendations well written, funny, and most of all helpful! Enjoy!
