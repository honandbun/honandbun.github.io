ABOUT US
She's Hon. He's Bun. Together, we're Hon + Bun!

A pair of Yelp Elites, we're always looking for the next best thing that food has to offer. We like all kinds of food: popular, hole-in-the-wall, you name it. We don't discriminate and we're coming to find you and eat you. Delicious food, you can run but you can't hide.

How did we come up with the name Hon + Bun? Well, we love nicknames! When we first started talking, we dabbled in a couple different nicknames but when we heard the words hon and bun together, we knew we found the ones. They sounded so well together that we thought it would be a good title for our page!
